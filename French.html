French

I am in advanced French class, which uses an immersive style of teaching where we only speak in French. It is an interesting class, and the teacher is understanding when students make mistakes in French, as long as we do not switch to English. We learn through various readings, films, class discussions, and projects. Every term we have to record our pronunciation of a short reading, and my recording for A term is shown below. To learn about French cuisine, our teacher had everyone choose a French dish to prepare and present upon. We had a potluck lunch, and all the food was delicious. I chose to make gratin, and my presentation can be viewed below or downloaded here.
