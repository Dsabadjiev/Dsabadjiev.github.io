Technical Writing

In Science and Technical Writing (STW), we learn to write and read technical writing. The class lines up very closely with STEM class, and many of our assignments for STW relate to our STEM I projects. For example, I took notes on scientific journal articles and researched other technical sources to gain background knowledge for the field of my STEM project: sonification.
